Objectives

The broad objective of the IIPC at KGEC is to reduce the gap between industry expectations (practice) and academic limitations by direct involvement of industry. Develop close connections between Industry and KGEC through interactions Bring practicing engineers at KGEC Disseminating technological advances through interactions and discussions. Training the students through continuing education programme. Publishing and issuing engineering bulletins and newsletters with latest technological developments. Organizing seminars, symposium, exhibitions and workshops.
